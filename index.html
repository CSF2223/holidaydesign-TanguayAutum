Krampus

Krampus

Heoller, Sophie-Claire, "Meet Santas Little Helper", www.insider.com, 12-5-17

Krampus is an "evil santa" who scares kids and takes the extreamly bad ones to Hell by throwing them into a sack or chains them; dropping them down a hole.

Gryla, The Thirteen Yule Lads mother

Gryla

Wikimedia, Creative Commons, by Andrii Gladii.

Gryla is the Thirteen Yule Lads mother, the lore behind her is that she hears whispers of kids misbehaving gathers them during winter and eats them.

Dead Horse Caroling

Dead Horse Caroling

Mari 2 - Ieuan Edwards

Dead Horse Caroling, or Mari Lwyd, is based in Whales. During December and January it is normal to open up the door and be greeted by someone holding a horses skull on a stick covered in a sackcloth.
